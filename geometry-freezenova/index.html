Geometry FNAF

By FNAF

Epic game Geometry Dash comes with new skins and levels in Geometry FNAF. Guide a little square on a spiky platform. Geometry FNAF is a 2D obstacle course game requiries quick reflexes using the one button control.

The Rhythm of the Game

Just like in the classic Geometry Dash, the key to success is to surrender to the music's beat. Turn up the volume, feel the rhythm, and get ready to bounce your way through obstacles!

The fast-paced and unforgiving nature of Geometry FNAF will force you to improve each time you play.Â Ready, Set, Bounce!

Controls

  • Use the W key, Space key or the upper arrow key to jump

How to Play

  • Start by choosing your game mode from our selection of 10 unique themes.
  • Then, your goal is simple: move your cube as far as possible along the platform without touching any spiky objects. Easy, right? Not quite!

Game Modes Galore

Each game mode presents a new challenge, from the frantic pace of Spikes Frenzy to the mind-bending chaos of Gravity Chaos. Here's a sneak peek at what you can expect:

  • Spikes Frenzy: A classic mode where you avoid obstacles and keep moving forward.
  • Glowing Boost: Add some extra bounce to your cube with glowing boost pads!
  • Orbital Thrust: Use orbits to escape spiky rows and keep moving.
  • Gravity Chaos: Defy gravity and navigate through golden halos that turn the platform upside down! But don't get too comfortable - gravity can change direction at any moment, keeping you on your toes.
  • Universe of Chaos: A universe filled with strange sights. From swirling vortexes to gravitational waves, it'll push your skills to the limit.
  • Blue Rush: Ride the wave of speed as you blast through this high-octane mode. With blue-tinted levels and boost pads, you'll feel like you're flying!
  • Jumping: Get back to basics with this classic Geometry FNAF mode. Jump, dodge, and soar through levels that require precision timing and quick reflexes.
  • Black & White: Only the shadows with this monochromatic mode. Black-and-white levels will challenge your visual skills and force you to rely on your instincts.
  • Flash Frenzy: A strobe-light effect like no other! Flashing lights and pulsating colors will make it tough to focus - but that's exactly what makes this mode so challenging.
  • Final Lap: Take on the ultimate challenge in this final showdown. Every obstacle, every spike, every boost - it all counts towards your ultimate goal: perfection.
