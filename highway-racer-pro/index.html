Highway Racer Pro

By FNAF

Highway Racer Pro is a premium online driving game where you can race on a crowded highway. With an insane amount of tuning options, you can customize your ride as you like. Want to upgrade your car's engine? Done! Want to change the color of your wheels? Easy peasy!

How to Play

  • Choose your car from our garage and get ready to hit the road.
  • Select from three game modes: One Way, Two Way, Time Attack, Speed Bomb, and Multiplayer.
  • In one way, cruise down the highway without any obstacles.
  • In two ways, drive in the opposite direction for extra cash and points.
  • In Time Attack, see how long you can drive within the time limit.
  • And in Speed Bomb, avoid driving lower the speed limit or risk having your car explode!

Controls

Use the following controls to navigate your way through the game:

  • Gas: W or up arrow
  • Steer: AD or left and right arrow keys
  • S key or Spacebar: Brake
  • C: Change camera
  • Left Shift: Nitro
  • Esc: Pause
  • K: Honk
  • L: Toggle headlights
