Nova Clicker

By FNAF

An interstellar journey begins in Nova Clicker. It's a game where stars are your currency, and clicking is your gateway to cosmic glory. Guided by FNAF, the charming mascot of FNAF.com, your mission is to unlock Novas, generate stars, and explore the reaches of spaceâ€”all with just a click or tap!

Your journey begins with FNAF himself. Every click brings you closer to building your own galactic empire. As you progress, you'll unlock unique versions of Novas. From Police Nova (who doesn't arrest you but might give you a ticket for not clicking fast enough) to King Nova (who reigns over your clicker empire with a royal nod), each new Nova adds a twist. And the adventure doesn't stop there! With dynamic backgrounds like the Moon, Mars, and Star Rain, the universe truly comes alive, giving you more reasons to keep clicking. And if you're lucky, Golden Novas might appear. Catch them to claim a massive bonus.

Controls

Computers

Click on FNAF to generate stars.

Mobiles

Tap on FNAF to create stars.

How to Play Nova Clicker

  • Begin with FNAF and click away to generate your first batch of stars.
  • Use your stars to purchase clickers and boosters from the shop on the right side of the screen. Upgrades include powerful items like the Nova Booster, Nova Bomb, and Nova Asteroid.
  • As your star count grows, unlock additional Novas with unique abilities, such as Baby Nova, Police Nova, and King Nova. Each Nova boosts your star production and adds an element of fun.
  • Watch for the occasional Golden Novas zipping across your screen. Clicking on them rewards you with an enormous influx of stars.
  • For those who love rapid progress, hit the x2 button to double your clicking speed and skyrocket your star production.
  • Progressing in the game unlocks vibrant backgrounds like the Moon, Mars, Star Rain and many more.

Ascend Your Novas

When you've unlocked most items and reached the peak of star production, it's time to ascend. Ascending resets your game, but don't worryâ€”you'll retain powerful bonuses and benefit from an ascension multiplier. This feature allows you to restart your cosmic journey with an advantage. It's like the universe hit the 'refresh' button, but this time, you're starting with a bigger bang.

If you're into online idle games and have ever wondered what it's like to manage a star factory, Nova Clicker is your ticket to the stars. Enjoy your journey through the galaxy!
