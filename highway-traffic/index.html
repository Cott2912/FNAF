Highway Traffic

By FNAF

Highway Traffic is a classic driving game that takes you back to the excitement of 90s arcade racing while offering modern twists. Whether you're zooming past competitors or executing daring maneuvers, this game delivers endless fun and challenges for both beginners and seasoned gamers.

In Highway Traffic, your goal is to navigate through dense traffic at high speeds. Here, you can:

  • Cut through Traffic: Overtake cars to score points. The riskier your move, the higher your score!
  • Perform Maneuvers: Dodge traffic with skillful steering or perform risky overtakes to double your points.
  • Upgrade Your Ride: Earn points to buy new cars or enhance your current vehicle with better features for superior performance.

Controls

  • W or up arrow: accelerate
  • A/D, or left/right arrow: steer the car
  • Space bar: use handbrake
  • Left shift or F: use car boost

Maps and Modes

  • One Way: Drive in a single direction against oncoming traffic.
  • Two-way: Navigate through traffic coming from both directions.
  • Time Attack: Race against the clock in a timed challenge.
  • Speed Bomb: A high-adrenaline mode where speed is key to avoiding explosions.

Weather Options

  • Sunny: Clear skies for maximum visibility.
  • Rainy: Wet roads add a layer of difficulty with slippery conditions.
  • Night: Dim lighting requires careful driving.

Why Play Highway Traffic?

  • Timeless Fun: Combines nostalgia with modern Unity 3D gaming dynamics.
  • Skill Development: Enhances quick decision-making and hand-eye coordination.
  • Replay Value: With multiple game modes, weather conditions, and upgrade options, each playthrough can offer a different experience.

What is Highway Traffic?

How can I improve my car in Highway Traffic?

Earn points by performing well in races, which you can then use in garage to buy new cars or upgrade your existing ones for better performance, speed, and handling.

Is Highway Traffic suitable for young players?

Yes, the game is designed to be accessible to players as young as 7. The controls are simple, and the game helps in developing quick reflexes and decision-making skills.

Why is Highway Traffic so popular?

Its blend of retro arcade feel with modern gameplay mechanics, along with a variety of modes and weather conditions, provides a fresh experience each time you play.

Can I play Highway Traffic offline?

Unfortunately, browser games like Highway Traffic require an internet connection to load and play. 

How does playing Highway Traffic improve skills?

It encourages players to think quickly, plan their route through traffic, and react swiftly, which can translate to improved coordination and decision-making in real-life scenarios.
