ArmedForces.io

By FNAF

A worldwide battle in the arena! Team up with friends and go hand in hand against terrorists! ArmedForces.io is a multiplayer FPS game where you get to be a part of a squad specially trained for a battle against invaders! Choose between many guns, including shotguns, sniper rifles and many more. Arrange your loadout, strategize and start the action!

Developer

ArmedForces.io is developed by FNAF.

Controls

  • WASD or arrow key to move
  • Left mouse click to fire
  • Right mouse click to aim
  • Space to slide while moving
  • Left shift to run
  • 1, 2, 3 to change weapon
  • E or F to interact / pick up weapon
  • G to throw available dynamite
  • H to use available medkit
  • T to chat
  • L to toggle cursor lock
  • C to crouch
  • Hold N to show scoreboard
  • Escape to pause

How to Play ArmedForces.io

To get started, follow these simple steps:

  • ArmedForces.io offers a range of weapons, including shotguns, sniper rifles, and machine guns. Choose the ones that fit your playstyle and strategy.
  • You can customize your loadout by selecting the weapons you want to use and arranging them in the order you want to switch between them.
  • Once you've set up your loadout, join a battle by clicking the "Battle" button. You can choose from different game modes, including Team Deathmatch, Free For All, and Capture the Flag.
  • ArmedForces.io has a variety of environments, including cities, forests, and deserts. Use these environments to your advantage by setting up ambushes, hiding behind walls, and flanking your enemies.
  • If you're playing with a team, communicate with your teammates to coordinate your strategy and work together to achieve victory.
  • Keep moving and avoid getting hit by staying close to cover and using your surroundings to evade enemy fire.
  • The more you play, the better you'll get. Practice your aiming skills, learn how to use your weapons effectively, and develop your own strategy.
