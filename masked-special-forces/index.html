Masked Special Forces

By FNAF

The battle on the field begins in a few minutes. With Masked Special Forces, you can jump into the action instantly. No need to download or install, just load the game in seconds and start playing! Grab your gun, wear your armor, pick a setting, and start shooting! This web-based multiplayer FPS game with maps inspired by CS:GO offers lots of customization features and realistic gameplay, ending your desperate search for a free premium shooter game.Â 

In the world of online multiplayer first-person shooter games, Masked Special Forces stands out as a true champion. With its vast array of customization options, you'll be able to tailor your gaming experience to your liking. From the moment you start playing, you'll be transported to a battle arena where you'll need to use your strategic skills to take down opponents one by one.

Controls

  • WASD or arrow keys to move
  • Left mouse click to fire
  • Right mouse click to aim
  • Space to jump
  • Q to slide while running
  • Left shift to run
  • 1, 2, 3 to change weapon
  • E or F to interact / pick up weapon
  • G to throw available dynamite
  • H to use available medkit
  • T to chat
  • L to toggle cursor lock
  • C to crouch
  • Hold Tab to show scoreboard
  • Escape to pause

Game Modes

With six different game modes to choose from, including Team Death Match, Free For All (Battle Royale), and Capture the Flag, there's something for every type of gamer.

Game Room of Your Own

Before the gunfight begins, you'll have the opportunity to customize your experience thoroughly. Select between available rooms, or create your own by selecting between detailed maps. You'll also have access to an array of upgradable weapons, armory, kill messages, and victory dances on the main menu.

With four different maps to choose from, each with realistic visuals and hidden gems, you'll be able to find your favorite and create your own room. You'll also have access to an array of weapons, including shotguns, rifles, and melees.

How to Play Masked Special Forces

  • To start playing, simply join a room made by other players or create one yourself. Once you've entered a room, the battle begins immediately.
  • Take your position and start eliminating enemies. Use your controls effectively, including WASD or arrow keys to move, left mouse click to fire, and right mouse click to aim.
  • In the profile section, you'll be able to change your nickname and load/save code. You'll also be able to view your stats, including K/D Rate, Games Played, Total Play Time, Kills, Scores, Wins, Headshot Counts, Knife Kills, and Assist Counts.
