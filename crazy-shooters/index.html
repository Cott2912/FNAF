Crazy Shooters

By FreezeNova

Enter Crazy Shooters, a 3D shooting game where speed and accuracy are essential. Move swiftly, shoot enemies on sight, and aim for the highest kill count to prove your skills. Customize game rooms, choose from maps like Factory and Arena, and select weapons such as Deagle and RPG. Track your performance with kill feeds, health indicators, and game statistics to dominate the battlefield. All free without signing up on FreezeNova.com!

Controls

  • WASD or arrow keys to move
  • Left mouse button to fire
  • Right mouse button to aim
  • R to reload
  • Space to jump
  • Hold shift to run
  • 1-9 key to change weapon
  • T to open chat and Enter to send
  • C to crouch

How to Play

  • In this game, you'll need to adapt quickly, move fast, and jump into action to take down enemies that appear in your sights. It's a game that requires strategy, skill, and a bit of luck.
  • Select from a range of weapons, each with its own unique characteristics. From the ballistic knife to the M87T, you'll need to find the right tool for the job to succeed.
  • Before you start playing, you'll need to create or join a room. Invite friends over for a multiplayer experience, or go solo and take on the challenge by yourself.
  • Crazy Shooters features four maps: Factory, Arena, Towers, and GasPlant. Each map offers a unique experience and challenges to overcome.
  • As you play, you'll be able to track your progress and stats in the game's statistics section. See how many games you've played, how many wins you've achieved, and how many kills you've scored.
