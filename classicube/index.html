ClassiCube

By FreezeNova

Do you want to dig the center of the world? Dive into the wild with green meadows, deep waters and the blueest sky you’ve ever seen! This setting comes with a single twist;  nothing has curves here! It’s all cubes and blocks.

ClassiCube is an online Minecraft game where you spawn onto a map of a diverse landscape. As the game is meant to be a playground for creativity and exploration, there are no life-threatening dangers here. No wild animals, no hunger to worry about. You have endless sources of blocks. Play in this peaceful environment, and build your own castle, town or anything your heart desires. That's what ClassiCube is all about - a sandbox game that lets you unleash your creativity.

Developer

Classicube is developed by FreezeNova.

Controls

  • WASD: Move
  • Left-Shift: Run
  • Left-Mouse Click: Break
  • Right-Mouse Click: Place
  • Z: Fly
  • Q/E: Move up/down while flying
  • X: Glitch through structures
  • Space: Jump
  • G: Drop blocks
  • B: Open block menu
  • T: Open chat menu
  • R: Spawn
  • Enter: Set spawn point
  • 1-9: Switch blocks
  • Mouse Wheel: Change blocks
  • ESC: Open menu

Unlimited Blocks

With ClassiCube, you'll have access to lots of blocks that you can use to build and shape your virtual world. From stones and grasses to dirt, cobblestones, and woods, you'll have endless possibilities to create your own unique building. Whether you want to build a castle, a cottage, or a whole new city, the choice is yours. Take a look at some of your block options in the image below.

Game Settings

  • You can customize your game settings to suit your preferences, from graphic options to chat options.
  • You can also generate new levels for yourself, choosing from two map options: Flatgrass and Vanilla.
  • With no enemies or wild animals to worry about, you can focus on building and creating without distractions.
