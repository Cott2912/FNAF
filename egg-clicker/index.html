Egg Clicker

By FNAF

Hey there, fellow gamers! Are you ready to join in on the Easter egg-stravaganza? Egg Clicker is the perfect way to celebrate the season with a fun idle game that's all about collecting and trading eggs.

Getting Started

  • To get started, simply click on an egg to produce more eggs.
  • As you progress, you'll unlock special boosters that'll help you earn even more eggs per click and per second.
  • From unique clickers like the "Easter Basket" to researchers that produce millions of eggs per second, you'll find the perfect booster to take your egg game to the next level!

The Fun Never Stops in egg-clicking

As you play, you'll discover new eggs with different colors, patterns, and characters like bunnies, gangsters, and even aliens! Each egg has its own unique skin and abilities.

Catch golden eggs for bonus points, achieve streaks for double production, and watch ads for instant boosts. The possibilities are endless!

Why You'll Love Egg Clicker

  • Play on any device, even where gaming websites are blocked!
  • Collect and trade thousands of eggs with unique skins and abilities
  • Unlock new boosters to upgrade your egg game and earn more rewards
  • Catch golden eggs for bonus points and rewards
  • Achieve streaks for double production and instant boosts
  • Enjoy a fun, idle game with endless possibilities!
