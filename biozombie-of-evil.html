Biozombie of Evil

By Unblocked Games FreezeNova

Get ready to fight with the zombies! Biozombie of Evil is an unblocked shooting game where your primary mission is to clear the city from evil creatures. They will be coming towards you recklessly. If they get close to you, they will hurt you. Don't let it happen. Shoot every zombie on the streets until there is none in this action game!

Storyline of Biozombie of Evil

  • The explosion of a nuclear power plant caused an unidentified chemical epidemic. This chemical turned everyone exposed to it into walking dead.
  • You are the only one left as a human. Protect yourself from battle hordes of undead in this shooting game.
  • Don't let them turn you into an evil. Dodge their rigid attacks. Improve your precise aiming capability.
  • You must eliminate a specific number of zombies in each level. To pass a new level, you must fulfill a task.
  • Start with killing ten zombies. This figure will increase at every level. Things will get a bit more like The Walking Dead!

Run Over Zombies by Car

You will encounter a police car in the city. It can be a natural shelter for you. You can hide from the masses of the walking dead by entering it. Or, you can drive it and crash it over. In addition, you can shoot at them while driving your car.

However, remember that the only car you can use in this thrilling action game is this police car. You can't use other ones. Find and use it for your benefit. You can enter it by using the F key. Don't let their rotten flesh scare you, as the car will guard you!

Officer of the Special Forces

Your most significant advantage in this epidemic that affects the whole world is that you are a special forces officer. You know how to fight with the bad ones and use weapons of many kinds. In the beginning, you have a Glock, a crowbar and grenades. As you progress, you will unlock new weapons, such as a revolver and a shotgun. Try to come to the last level and discover other weapons at your disposal in the zombie game.

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move
  • Left-Click: Shoot
  • Right-Click: Aim
  • Spacebar: Jump
  • Left-Shift: Run
  • T: Slow Motion
  • R: Reload
  • G: Grenade
  • V: Melee
  • H: Stand Tall
  • F: Enter Car
  • Tab: Pause
  • Mouse Scroll: Switch Weapons

Developer

G55 developed the Biozombie of Evil.
