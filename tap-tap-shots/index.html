Tap Tap Shots

By FreezeNova

One button, endless shots...Â Are you ready to dunk your way to the top in Tap Tap Shots, the addictive one-button basketball game that's a blast to play on your browser?

In this simple yet challenging game, you'll tap anywhere on the screen to jump the ball. Adjust its speed and angle to get it into the hoop. The goal is to score as many points as possible within a limited time. Don't worry if you miss - just keep tapping and jumping to make up for lost time.

Controls

Tap or click to dunk the ball

How to Play Tap Tap Shots

  • Tap or click anywhere on the screen to jump the ball.
  • The more you click, the higher the ball goes up.
  • Score before time runs out.
  • Make a chain with good scores and earn double points.
  • If you miss a single basket you should start from the beginning.

Aim for Perfection in Shots

One of the things that sets Tap Tap Shots apart from other sports games is its sense of rhythm. You'll find yourself getting into a groove as you tap out shots, and before you know it, you'll be on a roll. And when you're on fire, there's no feeling quite like it.

To take your game to the next level, aim for perfection by passing the ball through the center of the basket at a good angle to earn double points. Chain together successive perfect shots and watch your score skyrocket.

Who Created Tap Tap Shots?

Tap Tap Shots was created by Qky Games.

Play Anywhere, Anytime

Tap Tap Shots is fully compatible with Android, iOS, Chromebook, and PC devices. And the best part? It's a free online game, so you can play it anywhere, anytime, without any charge.
