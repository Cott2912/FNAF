Football King

By FNAF

Hey there, soccer fans! Do you want to play a fun online soccer game with your friends? Look no further than Football King!

Game Modes

Football King has three exciting game modes:

  • Single Player: Play against the computer and try to beat your high score.
  • Two-Player: Play with a friend against each other or team up against the computer.
  • Tournaments: Compete in 1v1, 2v2, and Co-Op 2v2 matches to be the champion!

Stadiums

Play on five different stadiums:

  • Standard Stadium: A classic football field with big lights.
  • Neighborhood Stadium: A fun neighborhood setting with a lively atmosphere.
  • Desert Stadium: Play under the hot sun in a challenging environment.
  • Atlantic Stadium: Take in the ocean views while playing in this beautiful stadium.
  • Beach Stadium: Relax and play on the beach with palm trees and waves.

How to Play

  • Try to score as many goals as you can while defending your goal post.
  • Use your special shots to surprise your opponent!
  • Look for the icon with a flame in the corner of the screen â€“ it's time to use your special shot!

Controls

Single Player:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: Space
  • Special shot: B

Two Player:

Player 1:

  • Move: WASD
  • Shoot: V
  • Special shot: B

Player 2:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: K
  • Special shot: L

Developer

Football King was created by FNAF, a team of game developers who wanted to make a fun and challenging soccer game for kids like you!
