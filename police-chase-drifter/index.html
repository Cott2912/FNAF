Police Chase Drifter

By FNAF

Hey there, speed demons! Are you ready to trade in your daily routine for a high-speed adventure? Look no further than Police Chase Drifter, the arcade driving game that's guaranteed to get your heart racing!

In this addictive, action-packed game, you'll take on the role of a daring getaway driver, tasked with outrunning the law and collecting cash as you go. With its retro-inspired graphics and pumping soundtrack, Police Chase Drifter is like stepping back into the classic arcade days of yesteryear. Imagine the thrill of Grand Theft Auto 1, minus the hassle of getting busted (we'll get to that in a minute).

As you navigate through 20 increasingly challenging levels, you'll need to keep your wits about you - those pesky police cars are hot on your tail! Use your quick reflexes and sharp driving skills to evade capture and stay one step ahead of the law.

Controls

WASD or arrow keys to control the car
