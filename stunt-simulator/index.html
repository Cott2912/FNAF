Stunt Simulator

By FNAF

Blocky cars riding on a futuristic playground. Hit the pedal and climb sky-high ramps at full speed. Have fun by crashing into AI players and ruining your car as you like. Or collect points from drifts, stunts and barrel rolls! Two different maps and 10 amazing vehicles are waiting to be tested.Â 

Stunt Simulator is a free online driving simulator game with crazy mechanics and 3D visuals. Play it on FNAF.com now!

Controls

  • WASD or arrow keys: Drive car
  • Space: Brake
  • Right shift: Gear Up
  • Left Shift: Gear down
  • Esc: Exit to pause menu

How to Play

  • Start by choosing a vehicle and map.
  • Use your car's gears and speed to navigate through ramps, tunnels, and jumps.
  • Drift, stunt, and barrel roll your way to collecting points.
  • Crash into AI opponents to add some excitement to your gameplay.
  • Keep track of your progress on the score display.

Game Features

  • 10 unique vehicles to test drive, each with its own strengths
  • 2 maps to explore, a realistic city or futuristic playground
  • Drift, stunt, and barrel roll your way to scoring points
  • Crash into AI opponents to add some excitement to your gameplay

Additional Settings

With these additional options, you'll be able to customize your gameplay experience and get even more out of Stunt Simulator!

  • Traction Control: Don't worry about spinning out of control or losing traction on the track. Traction Control is here to save the day! This feature helps prevent your tires from spinning or slipping when accelerating, braking, or cornering so you can focus on what matters - drifting like a pro!
  • AI Cars: Face off against some serious competition! AI Cars are computer-controlled opponents that will drive in a simulated way, trying to get in your way and challenge your skills. You can adjust their difficulty level to suit your style, making the game more or less challenging.
  • Aggressive AI: These AI cars will drive more aggressively, trying to cut you off and even crash into you if they can.
  • Manual Transmission: This option gives you complete control over your vehicle's gears, making it easy to execute complex maneuvers like drifts and stunts.
  • Drift Assist: Don't worry if you're new to drifting - Drift Assist is here to help! This feature automatically adjusts your speed and steering input to ensure you execute perfect drifts every time. Just sit back and enjoy the ride!
  • Stunt Assist: Perform some seriously sick stunts! With this feature, you'll easily execute complex jumps, flips, and barrel rolls.

By turning these options on or off, you can customize your gameplay experience and get even more out of the Stunt Simulator. Just hit the pause button while playing and choose the ones you'd like in your game. So what are you waiting for? Hit the track!
