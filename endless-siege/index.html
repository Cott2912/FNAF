Endless Siege

By FNAF

No, tower defense games are not dead! Endless Siege is a popular defense game where players strategically place turrets and javelins to protect the land from enemies coming in endless waves. Stop this endless siege by your strategies, upgrading your towers and rotating them to the boss enemies.Â 

Controls

Desktops and Laptops

  • Left-Click: Place Towers

Mobile Devices

  • Use the touchscreen to place towers.

How to Play

  • In this action-packed game, you'll need to strategically place towers to protect your land from never-ending waves of enemies.
  • The goal is to survive as long as possible by locating and upgrading towers.
  • Place towers in crucial locations to stop enemies from entering your territory.
  • Earn coins by eliminating enemies and use them to buy new towers or upgrade existing ones.
  • Place towers near entrance and exit areas to block enemies from escaping.
  • Upgrade your towers to enhance their damage rate, range, reload speed, and inflict power.
  • Set a strategy for each turret by choosing which enemy to target.

Towers

There are four types of towers:

  • Ballista: Deals damage and has a medium range (100 coins)
  • Torch: Deals moderate damage and has a short range (150 coins)
  • Cannon: Deals high damage and has a long range (250 coins)
  • Time Warper: Deals massive damage and has a very long range (500 coins)

Who created Endless Siege?

Developed by RavalMatic, Endless Siege was released in March 2021.
