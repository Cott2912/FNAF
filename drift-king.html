Drift King

By Unblocked Games FreezeNova

Do you want to satisfy your need for speed? Drift King is an unblocked drift game where you have the chance to perform stunning drifts! There are six racetracks with vivid graphics to enjoy. Prepare for going at full speed on these roads.

You can try to break the highest drift record on turning points. As you gain money, you will be able to unlock powerful sports cars in this car game. Acquire them and make the asphalt cry!

About Drift King

  • Choose your car from the car selection menu, and let's start! You can play this driving game in single-player or multiplayer mode.
  • There are ten sports cars that resemble the favorite vehicles of well-known car brands such as Ferrari and Porsche.
  • We believe that you will enjoy driving each of them on the racetracks.
  • Strive to unlock all the vehicles using your in-game currency earned by drifting. The more you drift, the more money you will get!
  • You can also customize your car's physical appearance from the "Garage" section.
  • Steer your beloved car on the endless roads in this drift game! Use every turning point to your advantage.

Tap on the NOS Bottles!

Each vehicle in this car game has a boost feature. However, you must activate nitro by tapping on the NOS bottles on the car selection menu. Click on the bottles five times and you will unlock the feature. Otherwise, you will not be able to use the booster.

Using the nitro will allow you to reach higher speeds and perform longer driftings. If you get a roadster, you will love feeling the wind on your face as you drive fast with it. Also, nitro can let you get ahead when competing with your friends in multiplayer mode.

Game Maps of Drift King

There are six racetracks in Drift King. Each comes with challenging bending points. While three of the racetracks are in oval shape, the rest are straight roads with turns and twists. Discover each racetrack with your car at high speed.

Don't leave any space unexplored. Blow your exhaust on these roads until you memorize every turning point of each of the tracks. You are also free to access the same racetracks in Multiplayer mode.

Controls

  • WASD or the Arrow Keys: Steer
  • Spacebar: Handbrake
  • Left-Shift: Nitro
  • C: Camera View
  • R: Reset
  • I: Start/Stop Engine
  • L: Lights
  • M: Rearview Mirror
  • B/N: Front Hood
  • P: Fullscreen
