Crazy Shooters 2

By FreezeNova

Explore Crazy Shooters 2, a tactical FPS game where strategy and survival skills matter. Create or join rooms in Free for All or Team Deathmatch modes. Navigate through 11 maps, including arenas and industrial zones. Arm yourself with various weapons like the Ballistic Knife, MP5KA4, and RPG. Monitor kills, health, and ammo on-screen, and compare stats with friends to track your progress.

Game Features

  • 11 detailed maps
  • 9 different guns
  • Online multiplayer
  • 2 game modes
  • Adjustable gameplay

Developer

Crazy Shooters 2 is developed by FreezeNova

Controls

  • Left mouse button to shoot
  • Right mouse button to aim
  • R to reload
  • C to crouch
  • P to pause
  • 1-9 key to change weapon
  • T to open chat and enter to send
  • Space bar to jump
  • Hold shift to run
  • Tab to open the menu
