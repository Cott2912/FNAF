Edys Car Simulator

By FreezeNova

Explore the city freely in Edy's Car Simulator! Choose from a variety of vehicles and roam the streets at your own pace. For a more immersive experience, play with the sound on. The test area in the game is a perfect option for adventurers seeking an unusual road with many obstacles.

Game Features

  • 5 cars in the garage
  • Test track
  • 3D visuals
  • Free to play on your browser

Developer

Edy's Car Simulator is developed by FreezeNova.

Controls

  • WSAD or arrows to throttle, brake, steer
  • Space for Handbrake
  • Enter to reset the vehicle (e.g. if it rolls over)
  • C to Change the camera
  • Tab or Page Up / Page Down to select a vehicle
  • 1,2,3,4,5 - to select a specific vehicle
  • E -  Makes the gray stone “jump”. Useful for load tests
  • R - Repair vehicle damage
  • T - Slow motion time mode on/off
  • P - Pause vehicles. Camera and vehicle selection can be used while paused.
  • Y - Show/Hide telemetry
  • Shift + Y - Change Telemetry mode.
  • Shift + Esc or Shift + R - Reset scene

Drive at Your Own Pace

It's a simulation that challenges you to master your drifting skills. Use the unique features of each vehicle to carve up the streets. Whether you're looking for long-term play or casually hanging out in a drifting game, you'll find plenty of opportunities to test your skills and learn new techniques.

Hidden Gem: Testing Track

The city in Edy's Car Simulator is full of hidden gems, from steep hills to sandy dunes. Testing track is one of them. You'll need to use your skills to navigate the challenging terrain in the testing track.
